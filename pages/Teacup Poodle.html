Teacup Poodle

Elegant Intelligence in Miniature Form

About the Breed

Origin

Developed from Standard Poodles through selective breeding in 20th century Europe

Size

2-4 lbs (Teacup), 4-6 lbs (Toy)

Temperament

Highly intelligent, affectionate, and eager to please

Breed History

15th Century

Origins in Germany

Descended from water retrieval dogs, originally bred as hunting companions

18th Century

Royal Favor

French nobility popularized smaller varieties as luxury companion dogs

20th Century

Modern Development

Selective breeding programs established the teacup variety

Key Characteristics

Coat

Hypoallergenic curly coat requiring regular grooming

Energy Level

Moderate energy with high mental stimulation needs

Trainability

Exceptionally quick learners, excel in obedience
